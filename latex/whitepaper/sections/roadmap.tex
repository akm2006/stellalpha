\section{Roadmap}

The Stellalpha development cycle is focused on achieving maximum execution stability while maintaining non-custodial sovereignty.

\subsection{Development Phases}

\begin{itemize}
  \item \textbf{Phase 01: Demo Environment (Completed)}: Full virtual-capital simulation proving copy models, sub-second latency, and intent replication logic without financial risk.
  \item \textbf{Phase 02: Strategy Synthesis (Active)}: Integration of the curated Star Trader registry with proprietary execution models and risk-optimized sizing parameters.
  \item \textbf{Phase 03: Non-custodial Pilot (Upcoming)}: Direct live execution of curated signals through secure, user-controlled vaults and precise allocation limits.
  \item \textbf{Phase 04: Protocol Expansion (Research)}: Deep infrastructure integration for complex DeFi interactions and the launch of a decentralized logic registry for multi-chain replication.
\end{itemize}
